Ingredients
===========
- 180 g (1½ cups) flour
- ½ tsp baking soda
- ¼ tsp baking powder
- ½ tsp cinnamon
- ¼ tsp nutmeg
- ⅛ tsp salt
- 1 egg
- 3 mashed bananas (1 cup, ≈280 g for kinda small bananas, ≈350 g for 
  normal-ish bananas)
- 54 g (¼ cup) oil
- 200 g sugar

Directions
==========
- Mix dry and wet ingredients separately.

- Combine, then pour into a loaf pan

- Bake 350°F for 55 min.
